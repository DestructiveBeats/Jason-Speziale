USUALLY 2 FEET LONG X 1 FOOT WIDTH X 4 INCHES HIGH , CAN BE POLISHED TOP , BALANCE ROCK PITCHED ( ROUGH SIDES AND EDGES ) OR IT CAN HAVE SAWN SIDES ( SMOOTH ) . A FLUSH MARKER CAN COME IN ANY SIZE AND FINISH . RESURRECTION CEMETERY REQUIRES A SMOOTH TOP ( NO POLISH / NOT SHINY ) AND SAWN SIDES , THE SIZE BEING 24 INCHES X 12 INCHES X 4 INCHES ( HEIGHT ) . THESE MONUMENTS ARE USUALLY SET FLUSH ( HENCE THE NAME ) WITH THE GRASS. THE FLUSH MARKERS COME IN A WIDE VARIETY OF COLORS
A MARKER WHOSE FRONT FACE IS CUT AT AN ANGLE / SLANT . THESE MARKERS COME IN A WIDE VARIETY OF SIZES AND FINISHES . THE TOP IS USUALLY A SERPENTINE TOP , BUT CAN ALSO BE A STRAIGHT TOP . MORAVIAN CEMETERY USES A 24 INCH LONG X 12 INCH WIDTH X 14 INCH HIGH SLANT MARKER FOR MOST OF THEIR SINGLE ( TWO PEOPLE ) PLOTS . ONCE AGAIN THEY ARE AVAILABLE IN ALL COLORS
THE FLANGE MARKER IS ONE OF THE MOST COMPLICATED MONUMENTS TO MAKE AS IT REQUIRES A FAIRLY GOOD SIZE BLOCK OF GRANITE AND CAN REQUIRE UP TO 10 DIFFERENT SAW CUTS WITH A DIAMOND BLADE ON A VERY EXPENSIVE SAW . FOR THIS REASON VERY FEW CEMETERIES ALLOW OR REQUIRE THIS STYLE STONE . THE BASIC SIZE IS 28 INCHES LONG 16 INCHES IN WIDTH AND 8 INCHES HIGH ( THIS IS SIZE FOR OCEAN VIEW CEMETERY AND GREY IS THE ONLY COLOR ALLOWED ) ONCE AGAIN THEY CAN COME IN ANY COLOR THAT YOU LIKE .
SINGLE UPRIGHTS COME IN A WIDE VARIETY OF SIZES ,FAR TO MANY TO LIST . THE MOST COMMON SIZE THAT WE USE IN RESURRECTION CEMETERY IS 22 INCHES LONG BY 8 INCHES IN WIDTH X 30 INCHES IN HEIGHT , THIS IS THE TOP PIECE ( CALLED THE DIE ) AND IT COMES WITH A BASE THAT THE DIE SITS ON TOP OF. THE BASE SIZE THAT HALL MONUMENTS USES IS USUALLY 24 INCHES LONG X 15 INCHES IN WIDTH X 6 INCHES HIGH . THE TOW PIECES OF GRANITE ( THE DIE AND THE BASE ) MEET THE MAXIMUM SIZE THAT RESURRECTION CEMETERY ALLOWS FOR A SINGLE GRAVE ( TWO PEOPLE ) - 24. INCHES WIDE X 36 INCHES HIGH . THESE MONUMENTS CAN COME IN A VARIETY OF SHAPES AND COLORS .
DOUBLE MONUMENTS ARE USUALLY FOR A DOUBLE GRAVE ( FOUR PEOPLE ) . THEY CAN START AT 30 INCHES WIDE AND GO TO AROUND 60 INCHES WIDE ( DEPENDING ON RULES OF INDIVIDUAL CEMETERIES ). AS YOU WILL SEE FROM THE PICTURES ATTACHED THE MONUMENTS CAN COME IN A WIDE VARIETY OF SHAPES , SIZES AND COLORS .
JEWISH SINGLE MONUMENTS CAN COME IN A VARIETY OF SHAPES AND SIZES . A TRADITIONAL JEWISH HEADSTONE WOULD BE MADE IN A GRAY GRANITE , BUT LIKE EVERYTHING ELSE THAT IS ALSO CHANGING , WE ARE SEEING MORE COLORS ( ESPECIALLY BLACK , BLUE AND PINK GRANITES ) BUT THE MAJORITY OF JEWISH HEADSTONES TODAY ARE STILL MADE IN GRAY GRANITE .
BRONZE MARKERS ARE NOT WIDELY USED IN THE NEW YORK METROPOLITAN AREA AS THEY ARE SOMETIMES TARGETED FOR THEFT . THE BRONZE IS USUALLY ATTACHED TO A FLUSH MARKER MADE OUT OF CONCRETE OR GRANITE . ALL BRONZE MARKER COLORS AFTER AWHILE WILL TURN TO A GREEN PATINA , WHICH SOME PEOPLE THINK THE LOOK IS QUAINT. OUR COMPANY CAN ALWAYS RESTORE THE LOOK BACK TO FACTORY SPECIFICATIONS IF WANTED .
DOUBLE SLANT MARKERS DO COME IN A WIDE VARIETY OF SIZES ( DEPENDING ON CEMETERY REGULATIONS ) AND SHAPES . MOST DOUBLE SLANT MARKERS USUALLY SIT ON TOP OF A GRANITE BASE . A TRADITIONAL SLANT MARKER COME WITH 3 INCH TOP NOSING AND A 3 INCH BOTTOM NOSING . MORAVIAN CEMETERY SOMETIMES USES A SLANT CALLED A WESTERN SLANT( IN SOME DOUBLE GRAVE AREAâ€™S ) , WHERE THE FRONT NOSING IS AT 0 ( WHICH MEANS BASICALLY A SMALL POINT ) AND THE TOP NOSING REMAINS THE SAME AT 3 INCHES . YOU WIL SEE EXAMPLES OF THE DIFFERENCES IN THE PICURES SUPPLIED .
JEWISH DOUBLE MONUMENTS ARE FOR NORMALLY FOR PEOPLE . ONCE AGAIN A TRADITIONAL MONUMENT WILL BE MADE OUT OF GREY GRANITE , BUT GRANITE OF BLACK , BLUE AND PINK ARE BECOMING MORE POPULAR . A TYPICAL DOUBLE MONUMENT WILL BE 42 INCHES IN LENGTH X 8 OR 10 INCHES IN WIDTH X 36 INCHES TALL. THE BASE WILL USUALLY BE 48 INCHES LONG BY 14 IN WIDTH X 12 INCHES HIGH . TYPICAL JEWISH MONUMENTS WILL ONLY BE POLISHED ON THE FRONT ,SMOOTH ON THE BACK AND ROCK PITCHED ( ROUGH ) ON THE TOP AND ENDS .
